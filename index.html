Navigating the Laryngology Fellowship Application

Welcome! This directory is designed to help prospective applicants explore the landscape of Laryngology fellowships. It's important to understand a few key aspects of the application process:

  • The NRMP Match: Most, but not all, Laryngology fellowships participate in the National Resident Matching Program (NRMP). This standardizes the timeline for applications, interviews, and offers.
  • Accreditation: Laryngology is a non-ACGME accredited subspecialty. Fellowships are typically approved by their sponsoring institution's Graduate Medical Education Committee.
  • Direct Applications: Applicants must prepare and send their application materials directly to each program.
